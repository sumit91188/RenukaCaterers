About Us

With a modest opening in 2013, Renuka Caterers is emerging as the Best Outdoor Catering Service in town. We treat every event in a special way and make it our priority to satisfy all your catering needs. Our prices are affordable and can be adjusted according to your budget. Since we are always up to date with the latest decor and catering fads, we can really make your event memorable.
